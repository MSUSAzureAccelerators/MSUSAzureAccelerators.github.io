Health and Human Services Datahub Solution Accelerator

Solution Accelerator

Provide Public Health and Community Service staff a decision support system with a unified view of the citizen utilization of services that a County provides. Unified data allows public health professionals to make well informed, data-backed, service level changes to maintain operation effectiveness and better react to spikes in service (from pandemic and/or other public health scenarios).

About this Solution Accelerator

A lack of integrated data impacts issues that government officials, data admins, and elected officials face daily. These issues include homelessness, behavioral health, fraud and abuse of Medicaid claims, recovery from the COVID-19 pandemic as led by the government, and economic recovery drivers such as 5G.

The Health & Human Services Datahub Solution Accelerator is unified, flexible, customized and allows quick, repeatable deployments that address unique challenges as quickly as they appear.
